Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to connect business owners in Galt with other businesses as a way of advertising and networking. A Chamber of Commerce help grow the businesses within a town and grow a sense of community among its city residence. This website is designed to do the same things for the city of Galt. Galt has a lot to offer new residents, and the chamber of commerce helps make these opportunities readily available to the city. In this increasingly digital world, it is vital that the Chamber of Commerce as an online presence.
