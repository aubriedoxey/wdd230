Target Audience

The target audience for this website is new or recently moved business owners. Galt is a growing town full of opportunities for new business owners and a great place for well established businesses to expand. Galt is a very community centered town. There are many locally owned businesses, and as such, the town largely supports other new, local businesses. There are a large variety of people and interests so Galt is the perfect place for any new business.

For example, if a new family moves into town and want to start a junk removal business, they could reach out to the Galt Chamber of Commerce to learn about more opportunities, when monthly meetings are, or how to become a member. Galt Chamber of Commerce is looking for ambitious people looking to improve themselves and their community.

People can also use this website to discover other businesses in the area.
